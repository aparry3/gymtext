Week 1, Day 1 — Squat Emphasis

Warm-Up:
- Treadmill walk: 5min easy
- Cat-cow: 10 reps
- Glute bridge: 2x12
- Goblet squat practice: 2x8 (no weight)

Workout:
- Goblet squat: 3x8 @ 15lbs
- DB bench press: 3x8 @ 15lbs each
- DB single-arm row: 3x8 each @ 20lbs
- DB Romanian deadlift: 2x10 @ 20lbs each
- Front plank: 2x30s

Cooldown:
- Walk: 2min
- Child's pose: 60s
- Cat-cow: 10 reps
- Hip flexor stretch: 30s each side

Notes: First session! Focus on learning movement patterns. Weight should feel moderate—you could do 3-4 more reps. Rest 90s between main lifts. Film goblet squat sets 1 and 3 for review.
