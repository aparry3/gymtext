Week 1, Day 2 — Hinge Emphasis

Warm-Up:
- Bike: 5min easy
- World's Greatest Stretch: 5 each side
- Glute bridge: 2x12
- Bodyweight hinge: 2x8 (practice)

Workout:
- DB Romanian deadlift: 3x8 @ 25lbs each
- Seated DB shoulder press: 3x8 @ 12lbs each
- Lat pulldown: 3x10 @ 50lbs
- Goblet squat: 2x10 @ 20lbs
- DB bicep curl: 2x12 @ 12lbs each

Cooldown:
- Walk: 2min
- Pigeon pose: 60s each side
- Seated hamstring stretch: 60s

Notes: Focus on hip hinge—push hips BACK, not down. Feel stretch in hamstrings. Keep back flat entire time. Rest 2min after RDL sets. Film all RDL sets for technique check.
