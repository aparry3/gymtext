Week 1, Lower 2 — Hinge Emphasis

Warm-Up:
- Row: 5min
- Cat-cow: 2x10
- Glute bridge: 2x15
- DL warm-up

Workout:
- Conventional deadlift: 4x6 @ 265lbs
- Front squat: 3x10 @ 155lbs
- BB hip thrust: 3x12 @ 225lbs
- Glute-ham raise: 3x8 (BW)
- Leg extension: 3x15 @ 110lbs
- Seated calf raise: 3x20 @ 90lbs

Cooldown:
- Walk: 2min
- Hamstring stretch: 60s each
- Figure-4 stretch: 60s each

Notes: Deadlift primary—posterior chain emphasis. RPE 7-8. Rest 3-4min on deadlifts. Congrats on completing Week 1!
