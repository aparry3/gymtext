Week 1 - Monday: Squat Day

Warm-Up:
- Assault bike: 5min easy
- Leg swing: 10 each direction
- Goblet squat: 2x10
- Hip circle: 10 each direction

Workout:
- BB back squat: 5x5 @ 75% (225lbs)
- Pause squat: 3x3 @ 65% (195lbs)
- Romanian deadlift: 4x8
- Leg press: 3x12
- Hanging leg raise: 3x10
- Plank: 3x45s

Notes: Rest 3-4min for squats, 2min for accessories. RPE 7-8 on main lifts. Focus on depth and staying tight in bottom position.
