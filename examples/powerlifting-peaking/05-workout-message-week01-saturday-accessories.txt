Week 1 - Saturday: Accessories & Recovery

Warm-Up:
- Row: 5min easy
- Arm circle: 20 each direction
- Bodyweight squat: 2x10

Upper Body:
- Incline DB press: 4x10
- Pull-up: 4x8-10
- DB row: 3x12 each arm
- Lateral raise: 3x15
- Bicep curl: 3x12

Lower Body:
- Leg extension: 3x15
- Leg curl: 3x15
- Hip thrust: 3x12

Core:
- Plank variation: 3x45-60s
- Farmer's carry: 3x40yd

Notes: Rest 60-90s between sets. RPE 5-7 - this should feel almost restorative. Optional: 10-15min easy cardio.
