Week 1 - Thursday: Deadlift Day

Warm-Up:
- Bike: 5min easy
- Cat-cow: 10 reps
- Glute bridge: 2x15
- RDL (empty bar): 2x10
- KB swing: 2x10

Workout:
- BB deadlift: 5x5 @ 75% (260lbs)
- Deficit deadlift: 3x4 @ 65% (225lbs)
- Front squat: 3x6
- Bulgarian split squat: 3x8 each leg
- Hamstring curl: 3x12
- Back extension: 3x15

Notes: Rest 4min for deadlifts, 2-3min for accessories. RPE 7-8 on main lifts. Focus on deliberate setup - don't rush. Reset fully between reps.
