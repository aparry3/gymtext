Week 1 - Tuesday: Bench Day

Warm-Up:
- Row: 5min easy
- Band pull-apart: 3x15
- Push-up: 2x10
- External rotation: 2x15 each arm

Workout:
- BB bench press: 5x5 @ 75% (132lbs)
- Close-grip bench: 4x6 @ 65% (115lbs)
- Speed deadlift: 6x2 @ 60% (208lbs)
- BB row: 4x8
- Overhead press: 3x8
- Face pull: 3x15
- Tricep pushdown: 3x12

Notes: Rest 3-4min for bench, 90s for speed deads, 60s for accessories. RPE 7-8 on main lifts. Practice comp pause on every bench rep.
